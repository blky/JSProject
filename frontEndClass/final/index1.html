  • Timeless familiarity

    The Eiffel Tower's wrought-iron spire, the Arc de Triomphe standing sentinel above Paris's most glamorous avenue, the Champs-Elysees, the gargoyled Notre Dame cathedral and the art nouveau cafes spilling onto wicker-chair-lined terraces are indelibly etched in the minds of anyone who's visited the city - and in the imaginations of anyone who hasn't.

  • All seasons

    France's romanticised capital seduces every moment of the year. Paris' cache of artistic treatures, chic fashion sense, exquisite cuisine and majestic monumnets captivate locals and visitors alike.

  • One of the great art repositories of the world

    In addition to big hitting museums including the world's largest, the Louvre, here are scores of smaller museums housing collections of every imageinable genre.
